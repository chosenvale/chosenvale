Schedule for 2014

All concerts to be held at the Mary Keene Chapel. Enfield, NH

Tuesday, June 17th. 8:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 7:30)

Marco Blaauw

Music by Stockhausen, con Bingen, and others.
free admission

Saturday, June 21st. 8:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 7:30)

Trumpet Calls

James Thompson, participants, and ensembles. Music by John Williams,Hovhaness, and others.
free admission

Friday, June 27th. 8:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 7:30)

20/20 Foresight

Including the premiere of Dartmouth Professor Spencer Topel's"Callings", an environmental work for 40+ trumpeters scattered around the grounds of the Enfield Shaker Museum.
free admission

Saturday, June 28th. 4:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 3:30)

Everybody Loves Difficult Music

Stephen Burns, Edward Carroll, Robert Sullivan, participants, andensembles. Music by Shostakovich and others.
free admission

Saturday, July 5th. 8:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 7:30)

Classic Percussion Music

by Lou Harrison, John Cage, brand new works by Tim Feeney,Marc Mellits, Caroline Shaw (2013 Pulitzer Prize winner), and more. Beautiful melodies and driving rhythms on drums, marimbas, vibraphones, tin cans, flowerpots, and anything you can hit.
free admission

Friday, July 11th. 8:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 7:30)

Virtuosity and Creativity

Music by our 2014 Pulitzer Prize Winning composer in residence,John Luther Adams, as well as Alejandro Vinao and Ivan Trevino. This concert will feature highlight the powerful virtuosic possibilities of percussion music.
free admission

Saturday, July 12th. 4:00 PM (pre-concert discussion at 3:30)

Festival Finale

The concert will be a celebration of the two weeks of music making atthe Chosen Vale International Percussion Seminar. It will feature works by our Pulitzer Prize winning composer in residence, John Luther Adams, as well as other fun and dynamic works by Ivan Trevino and Tristan Perich and will have a kaliedescope collection of instruments and sounds. So much to see and hear for the first time!
free admission
