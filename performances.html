The Chosen Vale International Percussion Seminar, 2017

All concerts to be held at the Mary Keene Chapel. Enfield, NH

Friday, July 7 at 8:00

Opening Concert

(pre-concert talk with Director Edward Carroll and Doug Perkins at 7:30)

Music by Chosen Vale favorites Johanna Beyer, Caroline Shaw, and Jason Treuting, and a world premiere by CV 2017 faculty member, Tim Feeney

Saturday, July 8 at 8:00

Resonance

(pre-concert talk with Director Edward Carroll and Doug Perkins at 7:30)

A celebration of textures and unexpected sonic combinations, featuring a rare performance of Stefon Gervasoni’s Bleu Juqu’au Blanc, new pieces by Paul Lansky, Kyle Krause, and more.

Friday, July 14 at 8:00

Chosen Vale Composers

(pre-concert talk with Director Edward Carroll and Doug Perkins at 7:30)

Music by 2017 composer-in-residence Tristan Perich, as well as new pieces and performances by Lesley Flanigan, Michael Burritt, and Ivan Trevino.

Saturday, July 15 at 4:00

Finale Concert

(pre-concert talk with Director Edward Carroll and Doug Perkins at 3:30)

We close this year’s seminar with two classics. The concert will open with Lou Harrison’s Suite and close with a full performance of Steve Reich’s Drumming prepared by Steve Reich expert and founding percussionist of Steve Reich and Musicians, Russell Hartenberger.
