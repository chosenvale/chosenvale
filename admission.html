Deadlines

There are no application deadlines for our seminars.

Only the first 40 accepted applicants will be enrolled. Others will be added to the waitlist.

The deadline for the Colin Currie Percussion Scholarship is February 1st, 2016.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Tuition Assistance

The Center for Advanced Musical Studies presents a unique educational experience and fantastic accommodations at what we believe to be a very low cost. Nevertheless, we recognize that some qualified individuals will be unable to attend our seminars without some sort of assistance.

Applicants who wish to apply for financial aid must include the following with their application:

  • two letters of recommendation

Colin Currie Percussion Scholarship

A $1000 scholarship provided by faculty member Colin Currie for a student demonstrating:

  • exceptional talent as a percussionist
  • potential as a leader in the field of percussion

Applicants who wish to apply for the Colin Currie percussion scholarship must include the following with their application:

  • a link to a video performance of two solos posted to Youtube or Vimeo
  • a 500-word statement of purpose.

Notification of Acceptance

Applicants will be notified of their status within 10 days of receipt of their application.

Accepted applicants must submit a tuition deposit to claim their position.

Forty participants will be enrolled in each seminar.

Some applicants may be placed on a waitlist.

Tuition deposits will be refunded to those who are waitlisted but not offered a place on the seminar.
